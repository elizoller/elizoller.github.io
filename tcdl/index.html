The Project

"Howdy, Mr. President!"

A Fort Worth Perspective of JFK

"Howdy, Mr. President!"

companion online exhibit to the physical exhibit

in the UT Arlington's Special Collections in Fall 2013

focus on JFK's time in Fort Worth

showcases over 80 photographs from the Fort Worth Star-Telegram

depicting JFK's visit to Fort Worth and the events following his assassination in Dallas, November 1963
create a sense of "walking" through the exhibit online if you couldn't make the physical exhibit
preserve the physical exhibit content for future access
have users access the content on their devices while in the physical exhibit

Responsive Web Design

What is it?

Responsive Web Design

  • a web design approach
  • optimal viewing across multitute of devices
  • adapts/responds to the viewing environment

Why?

Why RWD?

  • 57% of American adults are cell internet users
  • 34% of cell internet users mostly use their mobile device to access the internet

Pew Internet Study 2013

What makes it different from a mobile site?

No need to maintain multiple sets of content or pages
using a single url allows content to be shared easier and reduces load time by avoiding redirects
allows you set multiple breakpoints for a multitude of devices
