Gina

Gina is an artsy girl. By day she is a Spanish teacher writing lesson plans and taking cellphones hostage, by night she likes to paint and go to the movies. She loves sundresses and rarely matches her socks. Some favorite foods are chicken pot pie, asparagus, calmari, and chicken salad on crunchy lettuce wraps. She feels more blessed than words could describe that she found her adventuring soul-mate, Eli.

Eli

Eli is an all-around quirky guy. By day, he is a librarian building websites. By night, he enjoys reading sci fi, playing with their cats, and riding bikes. He has a passion for skinny ties and obsessively matches his bizarre (argyles, dinosaurs, and sharks) socks to his shirts. His favorite foods include broccoli, peanut butter, raspberries, and eggplant pizza. He feels incredibly lucky to have Gina in his life.
