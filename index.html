What has been accomplished so far?

Digital Collection Sites

Fort Worth Evolution

library.uta.edu/evolution

Bring together the historical photographs from W.D. Smith and the modern day photos

Team: Kris Swenson and Scott Holmes

Tech: CSS to create dynamic transitions of old images compared to new ones

"Howdy, Mr. President"

library.uta.edu/jfk

Companion to the JFK SPCO Exhibit- 80+ historical photos of JFK's trip to Fort Worth

Team: Candy McCormic, SPCO Staff, DC Staff (page-a-thon)

Tech:

  • Twitter Bootstrap framework
  • Responsive Design
  • Crowd-sourced memories

U.S. Mexico War

library.uta.edu/usmexicowar

Digitized SPCO materials from the U.S. Mexico War with content authored by historians

Team: Everyone, Project Manager- Lynn Johnson

Tech:

  • Database-driven site (PHP and MySQL)
  • Re-designed database, queries, and site structure
  • Converted content out of flash

Maverick Veterans' Voices

library.uta.edu/mavvets

Oral History Project- interviews with veterans

Team: Project Manager- Jeff Downing, Melissa Gonsalez, Kris Swenson, CD Walter, Candy McCormic, Evelyn Barker

Tech:

  • Database-driven site (PHP and MySQL)
  • Responsive Web Design
  • Streaming media content from Wowza

3D Printing Service

Tech:

  • Responsive Web Design
  • Server-side scripting with PHP
