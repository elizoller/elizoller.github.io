Google Analytics - what it offers

  • Visits per page
  • Language/Country/City of visitor
  • Browser, operating system, internet service provider
  • Real-time data and current active pages
  • Referral channels
  • Behavior flow - actual page hits and landing/exit pages
  • Load times
  • Site search usage
  • Events tracking - includes custom click tracking
  • In-page analytics
  • Conversion
